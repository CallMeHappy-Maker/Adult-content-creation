Platform Fees

Creator Fee Set by the creator for each service
Platform Fee 15% of the creator fee
Processing Fee 2.9% + $0.30 (Stripe processing)

Why 15%?

The platform fee covers the cost of running the marketplace: hosting, payment processing infrastructure, AI moderation, customer support, and ongoing development. This rate is competitive with similar platforms and allows us to maintain quality service for both creators and buyers.

Who pays what?

  • Buyers pay the total amount (creator fee + platform fee + processing fee)
  • Creators receive their listed price minus the platform fee
  • Stripe processing fees are passed through to the buyer

Refund Policy

  • Digital content (videos, photos): Refunds may be issued within 24 hours if content has not been delivered
  • Video calls: Refunds available if the session does not occur as scheduled
  • In-person bookings: Refundable up to 48 hours before the scheduled time
  • All refund requests are reviewed on a case-by-case basis
  • The platform fee is non-refundable once a creator has been paid

Payout Schedule

  • Creator payouts are processed after a 72-hour holding period
  • This holding period allows time for dispute resolution
  • Payouts require a completed profile and accepted legal attestation
